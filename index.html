About

Oliwia Jarząbek

I'm an AI video creator based in San Francisco, working at the intersection of cinematic storytelling and generative AI. I specialise in Higgsfield's Soul Engine — using SoulID for character consistency, physics-based motion for realism, and cinematic camera work to make AI content that doesn't look like AI.

My background is in social media, content creation, and visual design. I work with clients across European and US markets.

How I work

From concept
to final cut

Every project follows the same four-step process — so you always know where we are.

01
Brief & Direction
We nail the concept, mood, format, and platform. I build a visual reference board and agree on what "done" looks like.
02
SoulID & Assets
Character consistency locked with SoulID. Reference images, brand assets, and style frames prepared before any generation starts.
03
Generation & Iteration
Clips generated, reviewed, refined. Motion, lighting, and camera work dialled in until every second looks intentional.
04
Edit & Delivery
Cut to music, colour graded, format-optimised for your platform. Delivered as ready-to-publish files.
